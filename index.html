Introducing HOLOcomm

HOLOcomm is redefining how customers order and pay inside retail and food-service stores. We combine holographic display technology, AI voice interaction, and real-time payment processing into a single device that replaces traditional touchscreen kiosks and cashier labor.

Pilot units launching in 2026
